Understanding the National Education Policy 2020 and its career implications

What is NEP 2020?

The National Education Policy 2020 is India's transformative framework that reimagines education from rigid, exam-driven streams to a flexible, multidisciplinary, skill-based system. It empowers students to combine subjects across traditional stream boundaries, integrate vocational education, and pursue holistic development aligned with their individual competencies and career aspirations.
