About the Author

Mateen Yousuf is a dedicated educator with the School Education Department, Kashmir. He is passionate about integrating modern educational philosophy with India's NEP 2020 framework to transform how future generations learn, think, and contribute to society.

This simulator was designed to help educators, policymakers, and researchers model the long-term civilizational impact of education design choices.
